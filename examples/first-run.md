## Explainer · "mandatory binding arbitration clause"
> A mandatory binding arbitration clause means if you have a dispute with the company, you can't go to court. You must go to private judges (arbitrators) they choose… It helps companies avoid public trials that could set bad precedents or cost them more. It costs you access to the public justice system in favor of a private process they control… no matter how they word it, you always have the right to consult an attorney.
